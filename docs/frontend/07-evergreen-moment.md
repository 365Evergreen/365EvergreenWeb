# 🌿 Evergreen Moment – User Stories (signature section)

## 🔷 1. Core Story (brand differentiation)

### User story

**As a visitor**,  
I want to experience something distinctive and memorable,  
so that the brand stands out from other consulting sites.

### Acceptance criteria

* The section introduces a unique visual element (line growth)
* The messaging connects directly to the idea of “Evergreen”
* The section feels intentional, not decorative
* It creates a moment of pause in the user journey

***

## 🔷 2. Visual growth story

### User story

**As a viewer**,  
I want to see a visual representation of growth and structure,  
so that I intuitively understand the philosophy behind the service.

### Acceptance criteria

* A line animation evolves:
  1. starts simple
  2. grows
  3. branches
  4. resolves into structure
* The motion is smooth and slow
* The final visual suggests order emerging from complexity

***

## 🔷 3. Message story

### User story

**As a business decision-maker**,  
I want a simple explanation of why systems succeed or fail,  
so that I understand the value of design and planning.

### Acceptance criteria

* The message “Good systems don’t happen by accident” is clearly visible
* The follow-up reinforces intentional design
* The language is simple and insightful
* No technical explanation is introduced

***

## 🔷 4. Emotional impact story

### User story

**As a visitor**,  
I want this section to reinforce confidence,  
so that I trust the approach being presented.

### Acceptance criteria

* The tone is calm and authoritative
* The section builds on the outcome (not repeating it)
* The user feels:
  * “This is thoughtful”
  * “This is deliberate”
* No overt selling occurs

***

## 🔷 5. Motion restraint story

### User story

**As a user**,  
I want motion that enhances understanding,  
so that it does not distract from the message.

### Acceptance criteria

* Animation is subtle and slow
* No looping distractions
* Motion stops or settles into structure
* Works smoothly across devices

***
