What Is Power Automate and Why Should You Care?

June 20, 2026 · 6 min read

From manual chaos to predictable workflows

Power Automate is Microsoft’s cloud-based automation service that lets you connect apps, services, and data to create automated workflows called flows. Instead of manually moving files, sending emails, or updating records, you define the logic once and let the platform execute it for you.

It integrates deeply with Microsoft 365 (SharePoint, Teams, Outlook, OneDrive), Dynamics 365, Power Apps, and hundreds of third-party services like Salesforce, Twitter, and more via connectors.

Types of flows you can build

  • Automated cloud flows: Triggered by an event, such as “when an item is created in SharePoint” or “when an email arrives”.
  • Instant flows: Manually triggered by a button in the browser, mobile app, or inside other apps like Power Apps.
  • Scheduled flows: Run on a schedule, for example every night at midnight to perform clean-up or reporting.
  • Desktop flows: RPA-style automation that interacts with legacy or on-premises applications via UI automation.
